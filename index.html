Our Mission

Velocity Track Club is a student-led youth track and field program created to provide structured, safe, and enjoyable training for athletes of all experience levels. Our goal is to build strong fundamentals in distance, mid-distance, sprinting, and field events while promoting teamwork, discipline, and confidence.

Practice Information

  • Location: Cypress College Track
  • Practice Days & Times:
    • Monday: 5:00–6:30 PM
    • Wednesday: 5:00–6:30 PM
    • Saturday: 10:00–11:30 AM
  • Training Focus: Distance, mid-distance, sprints, hurdles, and jumps
  • Practice Structure: Group warm-up → Event training → Games → Cooldown & stretching

Training Groups

Athletes are grouped by age and experience to ensure proper pacing, safety, and individual development.

  • Group A: Younger or beginner athletes
  • Group B: Intermediate athletes with some experience
  • Group C: Older or more competitive athletes
